About Evan Campbell

Evan Campbell is a senior technology and business executive and management consultant. As an executive and entrepreneur, he has demonstrated success leading to two IPOs and two acquisitions by public companies.

He is currently Principal Consultant at Adaptivity LLC and Founder of Chinook Ranch Consulting, leveraging his experience in strategy implementation, OKR processes, Lean product development, and business agility to maximize clients' competitiveness and effectiveness.

Evan has held CTO, VP of Product Development, and VP of Professional Services roles in public, private, and venture-funded companies in several industries, had P&L responsibility, and has built and led organizations of hundreds of highly engaged knowledge workers.

He is a widely sought conference and keynote speaker, has authored many white papers and posts, and has done webinars with many thought leaders in strategy.
