Momentum Lab

From canvas to cadence.

Use this 4-step ritual to keep Adapt to Win workstreams alive between workshops.

01

Surface Signals

Collect weekly field notes, NPS verbatims, and ops metrics. Tag insights to the canvas they affect.

02

Reframe Options

Host a 30-minute huddle to re-map assumptions on the Lean Canvas and decide the next experiment.

03

Operationalize Change

Translate decisions into the Operating Model Canvas. Assign owners, dependencies, and enabling tools.

04

Measure & Celebrate

Review OKRs, highlight wins, and reset focus. Keep the diagram visible to reinforce system thinking.
